About This Website

YOUR TRUE COLORS is a project I created to highlight LGBTQ+ rights, struggles,
and victories across the six continents
—Africa, Asia, Europe, North America, South America, and Oceania.
Our stories deserve to be seen, heard, and remembered—every protest,
every act of love, every law changed, and every fight still being fought.
Too often, queer history is erased, overlooked, or told only from a Western lens.
I wanted to change that

This website is about amplifying LGBTQ+ voices from around the world—whether it's trans activists in
Uganda, queer artists in South Korea, indigenous two-spirit leaders in Canada, or grassroots organizers in Argentina.
Every place has its own journey, and all of them matter.
This is not just about history—it's about resistance, pride, and visibility.
It's about understanding how far we’ve come, and how far we still need to go.
I believe that knowledge is power, and sharing our global stories helps build solidarity,
awareness, and action

YOUR TRUE COLORS is for everyone who cares about justice, equality, and truth. Explore the pages,
learn something new, and remember: no matter where you are in the world, your identity is valid,
and your story is part of this global movement.
This is YOUR TRUE COLORS. Our stories. Our fight. Our future.
